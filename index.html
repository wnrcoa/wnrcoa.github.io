EN ‎

We have created our privacy policy to inform you about the protection of your personal data that you will provide to us while using 'wnrcoa', while accessing our website 'wnrcoa.github.io' and our 'WinningRoad Coa' discord server, within the framework of the requirements under the relevant legislation.

‎‎

By adding the 'WnR' tag to the beginning of your player name in the Curse of Aros mobile game, you confirm that you are a member of the WinningRoad guild. You expressly consent to the collection, storage, processing and use of your player name in the Curse of Aros mobile game, your 'Skill' and 'Combat' statistical information in the game, your 'High-Risk PvP zone' and 'PvP Area (safe pvp)' data 1 (one) year for the purposes of monitoring player and guild development, organizing 'League' and 'Cup' events, and sharing them publicly on our 'wnrcoa.github.io' website. If you leave the guild, your data will be deleted within 1 (one) year. In cases where the data is processed incorrectly or incompletely, your data will be corrected or deleted upon your request by contacting wnrcoa@outlook.com with your registered e-mail address in our systems.

‎‎

The personal data we collect through the contact form, including your name, email address, and discord name, as well as the data contained in the messages you send us, is collected solely for the purpose of communicating with you, responding to your requests, and providing our services. Your data will not be shared with third parties except as required by law. All necessary precautions are taken to ensure the security of your personal data. You can contact us to access, correct, or delete your data. By using our contact form, you share your data with us with your express consent.

‎‎

We store the data you directly share with us in your complaints and applications for the legal periods in order to fulfill our obligations under the Law No. 5651 on the regulation of publications made on the internet and the fight against crimes committed through these publications and the Law No. 6698 on the protection of personal data ("KVKK").

‎‎

Within the scope of Article 11 of the KVKK, you have the right to learn whether your personal data is being processed by contacting wnrcoa@outlook.com with your e-mail address registered in our systems, to request information about this if it has been processed, to learn the purpose of processing and whether it is being used in accordance with its purpose, to know the third parties to whom personal data has been transferred in the country or abroad, to request correction of personal data if it is processed incompletely or inaccurately, to request the deletion or destruction of personal data under the conditions stipulated in Article 7 of the KVKK, to request notification of correction, deletion, and destruction to third parties to whom personal data has been transferred, to object to the analysis of processed data exclusively through automated systems leading to a detrimental result, and to request compensation in the event of damages incurred due to the unlawful processing of personal data.

‎‎

Turkish Law will be applied in resolving any dispute arising from our privacy policy; Istanbul Central Courts and Enforcement Offices have jurisdiction.

‎‎

Changes to our Privacy Policy are communicated to WinningRoad guild members. If you are not a WinningRoad guild member, we encourage you to review our privacy policy periodically.

‎‎

‎For inquiries, requests, and complaints:
‎wnrcoa@outlook.com

‎‎

Guildden ayrılmanız durumunda verileriniz 1(bir) yıl içinde silinecektir. Verilerin yanlış veya eksik işlendiği durumlarda wnrcoa@outlook.com adresine sistemlerimizde kayıtlı elektronik posta adresinizle başvurarak talebiniz doğrultusunda verileriniz düzeltilecek veya silinecektir.

‎‎

İletişim formu ile topladığımız kişisel verileriniz olan name, e-mail, discord name ve bize göndereceğiniz mesajların içeriğindeki verileriniz, yalnızca sizinle iletişim kurmak, taleplerinizi yanıtlamak ve hizmetlerimizi sunmak amacıyla toplanmaktadır. Verileriniz, yasal zorunluluklar dışında üçüncü taraflarla paylaşılmayacaktır. Kişisel verilerinizin güvenliğini sağlamak için gerekli tüm önlemler alınmaktadır. Verilerinize erişmek, düzeltmek veya silmek için bizimle iletişime geçebilirsiniz. İletişim formumuzu kullanarak, verilerinizi bizimle açık rızanız doğrultusunda paylaşmış olursunuz.

‎‎

5651 sayılı internet ortamında yapılan yayınların düzenlenmesi ve bu yayınlar yoluyla işlenen suçlarla mücadele edilmesi hakkında kanun ile 6698 sayılı kişisel verilerin korunması kanunu ("KVKK") çerçevesindeki yükümlülüklerimizi yerine getirebilmek için şikayet ve başvurularınızda bizimle doğrudan paylaştığınız verileri kanuni süreler boyunca saklıyoruz.

League Rules

How many matches per player?: 2
Flask of Life usage: No
Win points: 3
Draw points: 1
Loss points: 0
Match may end in a draw: Yes
In case of a tie at the end of the league: The difference in wins between the players is taken into account.

Cup Rules

How many matches per player?: 2
Flask of Life usage: No
Match may end in a draw: No
In case of a tie: In the event of a tie, the players will meet a third time. The winner of the third match advances to the next round.

CoA and WnR Rules

1) No Harassment
Includes but not limited to, intentionally disrupting gameplay, targeting specific players based on ethnicity, language, or identity.

2) No Cheating
Use of third-party software to simulate or bypass human inputs. Recording and replaying actions, computer automated clicking, mechanical interaction, placement of objects on your device. All Inputs into the game must be controlled by a human user. Bans for this offense will extend to your alt accounts.

3) No Inappropriate Behavior
Sharing videos or photos of intimate acts, gore, or glorification of assault, terrorism, or racism. Using derogatory or disparaging terms, or verbal abuse, Intentionally bypassing profanity filters or abusing anyone with the intent to disrupt or intimidate.

4) No Bug Abuse
Any issue in game, that could be seen as unintended, or accidental, must be reported to staff immediately. Abusing bugs or glitches for personal gain is strictly forbidden. Sharing bugs or glitches with friends/players is forbidden. Warnings are not given for bug offences.

5) No Staff Impersonation
Pretending to be a Developer, Administrator, Moderator, Artist, Discord Moderator, & Guardian in game, or within the discord server.

6) No Scamming
Changing trade values, convincing players to “trust” you for wealth in return, claiming false or inflated prices are fair. This includes attempts at market manipulation and influence.

7) No Luring
Gaining the trust of another player and deceiving them by promise of rewards, publicity, or excitement in order to obtain their items. Be aware of merchant prices and changes to protected items within HR-PVP.

8) No Encouraging Rule Breaking
Convincing other players to take part in breaking rules, whether knowledgeable of rules or not. Player run mini-games that promote chat spam such as “counting” or targeted group harassment.

9) No Spam
Sending multiple, emoji’s, text, ascii, gifs, or images without context or enhancement to the conversation, whether they be identical or not. This includes reaction spam (reacting with random emoji's to comments).

10) No RWT
Real World Trading. The exchange or attempted exchange of in-game currency, items, services, accounts, details, guides, or secrets in return for real world currency or services is strictly forbidden. It creates a negative incentive to support the game and will not be tolerated. This includes currencies, favors, and goods or items from other games. (Buying and/or Selling)

11) Emulation
All inputs must be bound as 1 to 1 action. Singular Key press/hold outputs a singular in-game action.
(GOOD: numpad 5 = inventory)
(BAD: numpad 5 = attack and drink potion)

12) Advertising
Sharing games, YouTube, Twitch, in an attempt to displace CoA players and gain attraction elsewhere. YouTube or Twitch channels with the intent to grow an audience or profit. These activities must be done in a matter to share information, content or through similar interests and must be done in the appropriate channels. Under no circumstances should this be done within general chat(s) or in-game.

13) Account Sharing
Account sharing is not advised and is done at your own risk. We are not responsible for loss of items, progress, or the account itself. You assume the responsibilities of your actions as well as those you share the account with. Accounts are prohibited from being exchanged for any form of currency, whether it be items, services, gold, in game, or real world. It is recommended to have two factor authentication attached to any account you use in game. This can be done through your email host's website or account settings.

14) Respect CoA Staff
Staff members are to be treated with respect.
Staff members have the last word.
If you feel mistreated or poorly judged by any staff member, you may escalate your issue to me.

15) No Gambling
We will not condone nor respect the rules or activities of any gambling done outside of any purposefully-built systems of the game. This includes organizing and participating in unofficial gambling activity.
Ex: Gambling Discord servers, Guild Discord servers, personal trades with other players.

Failure to abide by any of these rules will result in a temporary or permanent suspension of your account, device, IP address, or a combination of the aforementioned. These rules are subject to change and may be edited without notice at any time.
